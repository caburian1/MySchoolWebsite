Public ComputersGuests have access to our libraries for public computer use and research while on campus. These computing labs have applications for word processing, spreadsheets, database management, statistics, electronic mail, and accessing the Internet. All lab Macs and PCs are equipped with the Microsoft Office Suite. Every lab computer has Internet Explorer, Safari or Firefox installed to browse the Web. Computer assistance is provided in the main labs.
Acquaintance Party @ CMSUAcquaintance Party in Caburian Memorial State University (CMSU) is one of the most anticipated events by the students. This activity allows the upperclassmen to welcome new students in the college, a time to mingle and acquaint themselves with one another. This makes them better students, as they will become more comfortable working and interacting with one another. The unpredictable downpour of rain which caused a very heavy traffic jam in the stretch of Dr. A. Santos Avenue did not stop the students to come on time at 7:00 o’clock on July 31, 2019 to party. The newly elected SSG Officers, together with the Dean of Student Affairs, Mr. Zernan Dimaiwat put their hearts and minds to conceptualize the theme and the program of the party. Fun-filled and remarkable are words perfectly captured the night of Denim in the Students’ Acquaintance Party.
